Bench Press

Bench press extend your body upper part.It is very easy to use just you have to lay down on flat bench and after that get one rode with weight plates and lift up with your arms.make sure don't try heavy weight if you are new.

Rowing Machine

Rowing machine very good for your heart and lunge.It also build your legs muscles, arms and upper body back. Rowing machine also build your stamina and burn your calories.simply just sit down on machine and put your legs on front foot pad and then pull the rope.

Treadmill

treadmill is very handy in your daily exercise.If you dont't want to run outside on the road then you can take that advantage from tredmill just you have to run on surface and you can control running speed and so on.It helps you to loss your weight bulid your stamina etc.

Chest-machine

Chest machine is similar to Bench press, but with the Chest machine you have to sit down and pull two small rode thing with your arms between your chest.It's burn your chest fat and build muscles.It used only for your chest and your back.

Dumbbells are great way to bulid your shoulders,triceps,biceps and chest muscles.you can use Dumbbells for chest just lay down on bench and lift up and for triceps and biceps just stand up and lift up Dumbbells and fold your arms and openarms.
